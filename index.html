— The Story

A land where
fire meets
the sea.

Banyuwangi sits at Java's easternmost tip — where the Indian Ocean meets the Bali Strait, and volcanic craters glow electric blue at midnight.

From the mythic Blue Fire of Kawah Ijen to the wild savannas of Baluran, this is Indonesia's best-kept secret, waiting to be explored on your terms.
